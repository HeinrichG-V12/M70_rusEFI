## NGK "coil on plug" coils:<br/> ## 

### NGK long version ###
- 48041: read head
- 48042: blank head
- overall length: 178mm

Pin out:
|Pin#|Label|Description|
|:----|:-----|:-----------|
|1|+12V|Power|
|2|GND|ECU Ground?|
|3|+5V|TTL, ignition signal from ECU|
|4|GND|Power Ground?|

### NGK normal version ###
- 48005: black head
- overall length: 165mm

Pin out:
|Pin#|Label|Description|
|:----|:-----|:-----------|
|1|+12V|Power|
|2|GND|ECU Ground?|
|3|+5V|TTL, ignition signal from ECU|
|4|GND|Power Ground?|

### NGK short version ###
- 48002: black head
- overall length: 140mm

Pinout:
|Pin#|Label|Description|
|:----|:-----|:-----------|
|1|GND|Power Ground?|
|2|GND|ECU Ground?|
|3|+12V|Power|
|4|+5V|TTL, ignition signal from ECU|
