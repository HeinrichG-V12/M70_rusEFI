## NGK "coil on plug" coils: ## 

### NGK short version ###
- 48003: black head
- overall length: 140mm

<img src="pictures/ngk_48003.jpg" title="NGK 48003 Short">

Pinout:
|Pin#|Label|Description|
|:----|:-----|:-----------|
|1|GND|Power Ground?|
|2|GND|ECU Ground?|
|3|+12V|Power|
|4|+5V|TTL, ignition signal from ECU|
