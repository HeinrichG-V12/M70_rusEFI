## relays for usage with Proteus ##

- main relay: 6136 8373700 (white green, make contact)
  - one input (terminal 30)
  - two outputs (terminal 87)
- 2x bank relay, 6136 6915327 (sky blue, make contact)
  - one input (terminal 30)
  - two outputs (terminal 87)
- wire size:
  - B+ to 30: 2.5mm²
  - 87 to splice: 2.5mm²
  - splice to coils: 1.5mm²
